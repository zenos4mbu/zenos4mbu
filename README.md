### 🔧 What I work on

- **Industrial machine vision** — camera-based acquisition pipelines, multi-view geometry, image-quality optimization for reliable, repeatable inspection.
- **Deep learning for vision** — real-time object detection (YOLO, DETR), 3D human pose & shape estimation (SMPL), 3D reconstruction (COLMAP, NeRF, Gaussian Splatting).
- **Deployment & edge** — taking models from data to production on embedded hardware with TensorFlow Lite and ONNX.

### 🚀 Highlights

- 🏎️ Author of the core 3D human-mesh reconstruction algorithms behind a **live demo at CES 2026 (Las Vegas)**.
- 📄 Published at **ECCV, CVPR, CVIU** — and a study on **3D reconstruction in industrial settings** (Ital-IA 2024).
- 🔬 Reviewer for **CVPR, ICCV and ICIP**.
